Help & Documentation

Getting Started

CinePrompt AI helps you generate high-quality prompts for AI image generators with a cinematic focus. Follow these steps to create your perfect prompt:

  1. Fill in the Subject field with your main subject
  2. Select a Genre and Setting that matches your vision
  3. Choose Style, Lighting, and Mood options
  4. Set Technical Options like aspect ratio and resolution
  5. Add any Additional Details to refine your prompt
  6. Click Generate Prompt to create your prompt

You can also click I'm Feeling Lucky to generate a random prompt.

Tips for Better Prompts

  • Be specific with your subject description
  • Choose complementary style and lighting options
  • Use the additional details field to add specific elements
  • Include a reference image URL for better results
  • Save your favorite prompts to reuse later
